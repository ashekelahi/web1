WHAT IS MUTUAL FUND?

IDLC Systematic Investment Plan (IDLC SIP) is a simple, convenient and disciplined way to make investment for fulfilling your life goals. Under SIP, investors invest a fixed amount of money every month for a specific time period in a suitable mutual fund available for them.


Mutual funds are managed by professional asset managers to attain the investment objectives of the investors of the fund.
